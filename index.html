Dan Plyukhin

I'm a postdoc at the University of Southern Denmark (SDU) in the ACP Section. My research aims to make distributed systems safer, faster, and more composable by developing programming abstractions and runtime support.

Mentorship

During the Spring 2020 semester, I had the pleasure of mentoring Jerry Wu and Charles Kuch for the Undergraduate Research Apprentice Program. Their project was to implement and evaluate the reference tracking scheme from my AGERE 2018 paper, as a first step towards distributed actor garbage collection for the Akka framework.
